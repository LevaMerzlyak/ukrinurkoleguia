Olha Danchenko

Attorney at law

Languages

English, Deutsch, Russian, Ukrainian

General information

Olha Danchenko joined Ukriniurkoleguia in 2001.

In 2009 Olha Danchenko was appointed Head of Australia and New Zealand Section. She coordinates the handling of estate matters in Australia and New Zealand and advises on probate legislation of these countries.

Since 2010 Olha Danchenko is in charge of the Family Law Department in Ukriniurkoleguia. Ms. Danchenko provides advice and legal opinions in matters of international adoption, surrogacy programs, international protection of children, alimony obligations.

Olha Danchenko is also the leading expert of Ukriniurkoleguia in legal support of insured events such as motor accidents.
