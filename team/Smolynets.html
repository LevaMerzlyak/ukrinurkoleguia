Daria Smolynets

Attorney at law

Languages

Polish, Russian, Ukrainian

General information

Daria Smolynets has been working in the Western Ukrainian Bureau of Ukriniurkoleguia since June 1983; in January 1990 she was admitted to the Specialized Bar Association "Iniurkoleguia".

For over 20 years Daria Smolynets is engaged in conduct of probate cases, associated with the historic region of "Galicia". Ms. Smolynets is a principal expert in the areas of probate law and legal protection of migrant workers of Western Ukrainian Bureau of Ukriniurkoleguia. She also advises on probate and family law.

Education:

  • Ivan Franko National University of Lviv, Faculty of Law
